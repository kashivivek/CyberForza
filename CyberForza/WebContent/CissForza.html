CISS FORZA

Did you know that 40% of employees have unnecessary access privileges? What about that over 25% of employees admitted to having retrieved information not relevant to their job like financial, reports, salary info, HR and personnel docs?


Gartner’s latest security survey outlines insider threats continue to be a serious ongoing concern for organizations. Employees are being granted excessive privileges and access resulting in unnecessary risks to organizations across the globe. 80% of respondents believe that it’s at least somewhat likely that employees has accessed sensitive or confidential data out of curiosity

Privileged user’s customer information is considered at risk without proper access controls for privileged users. To avoid such risks, CISS Forza provides security with proper access controls over privileged users.

CISS Forza Benefits

  • Integrated solution of CISS Interceptor with Privileged Access Management (PAM).
  • Protects, monitors and respond to malicious activities.
  • Delegating access to Privileged accounts.
  • Account access with proper governance and monitoring.
  • Eliminates the risk of compliance violations, privacy breaches, and fraud due to shared accounts.
  • Ensures compliance with regulations through preventive, detective and corrective controls as well as audit of the controls and access privileges.
  • Improves productivity and the user experience by automating the management of access to privileged and shared accounts.
  • Establishes accountability by enforcing consistent policies for acquiring access to accounts.
  • Eliminates the risk of anonymous logins to privileged and shared accounts regardless whether the systems and applications are on-premise, hosted or SaaS.
  • Enforces the Least Privilege principle by enabling access to highly-privileged accounts only when needed.
  • Provides agility to quickly accommodate changed business processes.
